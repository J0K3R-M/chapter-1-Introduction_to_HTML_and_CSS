Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

This classic chocolate chip cookie recipe is easy to make and absolutely delicious. Perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda; set aside.
  3. In the bowl of an electric mixer, beat the butter and both sugars on medium speed until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the salt, vanilla, and eggs; beat until well mixed.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture; beat until just combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop dough by rounded tablespoons onto the prepared baking sheets.
  8. Bake until cookies are golden around the edges, but still soft in the center, 8 to 10 minutes.
  9. Remove from oven, and let cool on baking sheets for 1 to 2 minutes.
  10. Transfer to a wire rack, and let cool completely.
